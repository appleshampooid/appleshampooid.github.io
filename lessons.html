Lessons Learned

Here are some lessons we learned from our project:
  • When you create a team made up of those people who were absent from class on the day the teams were created, you end up with a team made up of members with questionable motivation!
  • The divide and conquer approach worked well.
  • Gravity is best modeled when it works perpendicular to earth.s surface, not parallel to it.
  • Team spirit will not win competitions, but it makes them far more enjoyable.
  • Models are not inaccurate because of their nature, but their lack of understanding it.
  • Teams of the missing get lost easily.
What would we do differently if we had to do the project over again?
  • Start working on it earlier
  • Attend one of the testing runs (only one member made it to one of them)
